My Design Process

Discovery & Alignment

Concept Development

Next, we strip away the visuals to focus purely on structure. I’ll sketch out low-fidelity wireframes in Figma to define the layout and user journey.

03

Design & Refinement

Now, we bring the project to life. I’ll transform the wireframes into high-fidelity, interactive prototypes that look and feel exactly like the final product.
